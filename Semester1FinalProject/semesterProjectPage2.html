Meaning of letters in formulas

(Not all will be used in the formulas below)

  • F = force
  • m = mass
  • a = acceleration
  • x = distance
  • v = velocity
  • g = force of gravity
  • p = momentum
  • t = time
  • h = height
  • KE = kinetic energy
  • PE = potential energy
  • Subi = initial
  • Subf = final
  • Subair = air resistance
  • Subapp = applied force
  • Subnet = average force
